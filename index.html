About Vit Hostel 

                                      
                                     
                                                      The  VIT-Chennai  hostels  are  highly  secure  and  well  guarded.
                                                      Only  VITians &  VIT  authorized   personnel   are  allowed inside. 
                                                      We  aspire  to  make  the  hostel  a "home  away  from  home. Each
                                                      hostel  room  is  equipped  with  a  cot, chair, study table   and  
                                                      cupboard. Resident  wardens  are available  24*7. Provision stores 
                                                      are located in  the hostel where students can  buy essentials such 
                                                      as mattresses, pillows, buckets, mugs, locks, stationeries, books.

                                                      Both  AC and  Non-AC rooms  are  available.The  type  of room   is 
                                                      allocated  on the  first-come   first serve basis and availability
                                                      once the hostel  fee is paid full.

Hostels

  • 2 ,3 & 4 Bedded accommodation
  • Optional A/C facility from centralized plant with fresh air supply
  • State of the art Gym & Indoor games

  • 24/7 in house medical care unit and 24/7 ambulance facility available.

  • Entertainment halls with multi channel television facility
  • Open air movie screening facility for weekly movie shows
  • Exclusive visitors lounge
  • 24/7 security vigilance
  • Treated RO & Cool water in all the floors for drinking purpose.
  • Solar hot water for bathing.

  • smoking - free campus
  • Ragging - free campus

Dining

                                                   to  different  needs  of  students .We  have special, north Indian, south Indian, 
                                                   vegetarian, non-vegetarian categories of food served in different messes. Special

                                                   to cater to the needs of the students.
